How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Questly account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to: boxedfusion@gmail.com
  2. Subject line: "Questly Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include in your email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your username (if applicable)
    • Confirmation that you want to delete your account and all associated data
  4. Response time: We will process your request within 7 business days

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • User Account: Your complete user profile, including username, email address, and account settings
  • Location Data: Any location information that may have been collected during app usage
  • User Generated Content: All content you have created, saved, or uploaded within the Questly app
  • App Usage Data: Your interaction history and preferences within the application
  • Device Information: Any device identifiers or technical information associated with your account

Data Retention Period

Immediate Deletion: Most of your personal data will be deleted within 30 days of your deletion request.

Legal Retention: Some data may be retained for up to 90 days to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our terms of service. After this period, all remaining data will be permanently deleted.

Anonymized Data: Any anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be traced back to you may be retained for analytical purposes.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent - Once deleted, your account and data cannot be recovered
  • Service access - After deletion, you will no longer be able to access Questly with your current account
  • Third-party services - If you've connected third-party services, you may need to revoke access separately
